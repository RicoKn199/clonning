The MORE Family Collection

Our people on the ground create highly personalised experiences to bring you closer to Mother Nature, the greatest teacher about the only things in life that really matter.

Our purpose is to help you open your eyes and heart, and reconnect with how ‘wonder-full’ life is – whether in the company of loved ones or on your own.

We invite you to take a closer look at our Lodges and Hotels, found in some of Southern Africa’s most beautiful and untouched destinations. And to immerse yourself in our experiences. They'll enrich your life.

Safaris

THE WILD LIFE

This is where life begins. Our safaris connect you to pure Africa, ancient and unchanged. Immerse yourself in this wild kingdom (including apex predators, the near-extinct, and countless other shapes and sizes - each one unique and necessary to this balanced ecosystem). Extraordinary landscapes; amber sunrises and magenta sunsets; clean, cold rivers and fresh air... It's addictive.

Whether you game drive, bush walk or river cruise to take it all in, you are guaranteed a more private and personal experience in the company of our expert field guides and trackers. They add the human touch, enhanced by knowledge and coloured by personality.
